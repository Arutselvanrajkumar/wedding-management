Our Services

Consultation and Budgeting: Wedding planners can help you create a budget and allocate funds to different aspects of the wedding, ensuring you stay on track financially.

Vendor Selection: They can recommend and connect you with reliable vendors such as caterers, florists, photographers, and musicians, based on your preferences and budget.

Venue Selection:Wedding planners can assist in finding the perfect venue for your wedding, whether it's a hotel, beach resort, banquet hall, or something more unique like a vineyard or garden.

Event Design and Theme: can help you conceptualize the overall theme and design of the wedding, including color schemes, decorations, and other aesthetic elements.

Timeline and Logistics:Wedding planners create a detailed timeline for the wedding day, ensuring that everything runs smoothly. They handle logistical aspects like coordinating vendor arrivals, managing the schedule, and overseeing setup and teardown. On the wedding day, planners ensure that everything goes according to plan. They handle last-minute issues, guide guests, and coordinate with vendors, allowing the couple and their families to relax and enjoy the day.

Custom Services:Some wedding planners offer specialized services like destination wedding planning, cultural or religious ceremonies expertise, or eco-friendly wedding planning.

RSVP Management:Wedding planners can help manage guest lists and RSVPs, ensuring you have an accurate count of attendees for catering and seating arrangements.

Post-Wedding Tasks: They can assist with tasks after the wedding, such as returning rentals, ensuring vendors are paid, and handling post-event paperwork.
